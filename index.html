Rules

Purpose

Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower.

Gameplay

When the game starts you have 0 points and the number of wrong guesses left is 5. When you choose higher and the next card is higher, you get 1 point. Of course it is the same with lower. BUT when you say higher and the card is lower or vice versa, the amount of wrong guesses will be dropped by 1. The game continues until the number of wrong guesses left is 0.

Important note

We added a number 1 card so this is the lowest card and the A is the highest one!
